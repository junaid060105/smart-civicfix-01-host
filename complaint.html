Why only 3 reports?

To keep the system fair and responsive, each citizen is limited to 3 active reports at a time. Here's why:

  • Prevents spam and duplicate submissions
  • Ensures municipal workers can focus on existing issues
  • Keeps the queue manageable for faster responses
  • Encourages follow-up on reported issues

💡 Once one of your reports is resolved, a slot frees up automatically.
