Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 22, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when you use the Smart CivicFix platform to report an issue, create an account, or participate in the rewards program. This includes:

  • Contact details (Name, Email, Phone Number)
  • Location data (Geotags associated with your reports)
  • Photos and descriptions of the reported issues

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used strictly to facilitate the resolution of municipal issues. This includes routing your report to the appropriate department, updating you on its status, and awarding civic points.

3. Data Security & Sharing

We do not sell your data. Your data is shared only with authorized municipal workers and contractors necessary to resolve the reported issue. All personal data is encrypted in transit and at rest.

4. Your Rights

You have the right to access, modify, or delete your account data at any time via the Account Settings page.
